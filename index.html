Prime Toronto Location

Heart of The Annex

Located in Toronto's vibrant Annex neighborhood, you'll be at the center of student life. With quick access to Spadina and Dupont subway stations, you can reach any campus in minutes.

  • Walking distance to University of Toronto St. George Campus
  • Walking distance to George Brown College
  • Easy transit connections to Toronto Metropolitan University (TMU)
  • Chinatown's authentic restaurants and late-night eats
  • Steps from glitzy Yorkville's upscale restaurants and stores
  • Cozy coffee shops in the neighbourhood, perfect for studying
  • Close to Kensington Market's eclectic food scene
  • Grocery stores and daily essentials nearby
